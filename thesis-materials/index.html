Research Summary

Puzzle
Why are development finance and FDI so often collapsed into “Chinese investment” in Indonesian elite narratives?
Argument
Blurring is most likely when state involvement is visible but the underlying exposure is hard to audit.
Evidence
Project mapping, media coding of 762 articles, and elite interviews.
